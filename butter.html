Project type: Digital Product, Branding

Timeline: 4 months

Brief: Propose, research, and design a startup idea

Team: Cornell Tech Startup Studio
with Michael Shin, Arun Narayanan, and Sush Krishnamoorthy
Butter is a restaurant app designed to help users save, organize & recall restaurants seamlessly. Better tracking means better sharing, means better recommendations from the people you trust. Butter brings the social aspect back to getting recommendations, using a closed network system so that you only get useful reviews from the people that matter to you. Discover new restaurants your friends are interested in through the feed, look through custom user defined tags to see unique lists, or look through the lists of your trend-setting friends. Stay organized and remember the places you have been and want to go using tags. Butter makes food better.
