<   Bumblebee
Project type: Branding, Collage

Timeline: 4 weeks

Brief: How can create a clothing brand that gives back to the community?
Bumblebee Loungewear is inspired by those lazy days at home, spent barefoot soaking in the afternoon sun. The apparel is soft and luxurious, beautiful and romantic, without sacrificing comfort or utility. Bumblebee is for the modern romantic, the young woman who balances her hectic life with a lazy afternoon and a good book. It is for the dreamers, the thinkers, the lovers of literature, who want to give back to the world while enjoying a piece of understated luxury. The logo for Bumblebee is formed using the letters BB, short for Bumblebee. Each letter B represents a wing. The two letters move, change, and interact with each other, buzzing as a bee would.
