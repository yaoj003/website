<   Smartie
Project type: Digital Product, Game Design, Branding

Timeline: 3 months

Brief: How can an application encourage people to use their phones less?
Smartie is a mobile application that helps smartphone users cut down their phone usage with a fun and motivating game that anthropomorphizes the phone as a metaphor for our codependent relationship. Treat your phone as a pet: take care of it when it is hungry, tired, or messy and earn points to customize your Smartie with accessories.
