<   Sous Le Manteau
Project type: Branding, Packaging Design

Timeline: 2 years

Role: Graphic Designer for SLM

Visit product store   >
Created under the creative director Olivia Bransbourg, SLM is inspired by aphrodisiac potions from the Middle Ages, re-contextualized into a modern day love story focusing on enchantment and seduction. I created the logo and brand identity, expressing these in a website, as well as bottles and packaging for its line of fragrances, including a leaflet and ceramic pendant included inside.
