<   Bloom
Project type: Digital Product, Branding

Timeline: 3 months

Brief: Research a problem close to your heart and design a digital solution to solve it.
Bloom is the plant care application designed by plant lovers for plant lovers. With reminders, journaling, and on demand plant sitting services, Bloom helps keep plants happy and healthy. Bloom reduces the friction for finding someone to take care of your plants when you are not home, ensuring that plant owners can vacation worry free.
